Kono

A social application aimed towards helping people suffering from chronic diseases.

BACK

THE IDEA

Kono is an application that aims to improve the life of people suffering from chronic illnesses by providing them with recommended meal plans and scheduling excercises based on their profile. I was in charge of doing a visual design of the given User Flow.




EXPLORATION AND BRAINSTORMING

I was in charge of working on the design of a Shopping Cart, a News Feed that would show recipes/photos shared/added, a Profile Screen, a walkthrough setup and a login screen.
