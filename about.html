ABOUT ME

Product Designer. Aspiring Entrepreneur.

Resume

I am a design focused engineer majoring in Computer Science at The University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. I try to use my creative vision mixed with my technical knowledge and the amazing works that I have seen in my life to design and build better products and features for the users.

I have been working as a Product Designer for a startup based in Urbana-Champaign, Flying Superintendents, that allows workers at construction sites to track progress by allowing the users to visualize the development on a cloud platform using 3-D Models that are based off pictures taken by a drone.

I also started moonlighting as a Freelance Product Designer for Gigster where I work directly with clients to design and build products. I just finished working on the design for a mobile application that allows the users to track their daily meals and exercise schedules. The application was mainly designed for users suffering from chronic illnesses.

I have also volunteered for community service initiatives. This summer I worked for a non-profit organization where I worked on a new initiative of theirs meant to provide street children to discover and hone their talents and aimed to provide them with a purpose in life. In 11th grade I also worked on a project that would allow successful integration of underprivileged students into the top schools of the city. Till date, the model has helped over 2000 students get proper education.
I try to make as much impact as I can using my skills and background. I love to keep getting better at what I do and learning new things. I am not afraid to experiment as with each new thing you try you learn something else irrespective of the success or failure.
