FACEBOOK

Interned with Facebook as a Product Design Intern on the Design Tools team. I worked on Origami Studio, a prototyping tool and designed a feature that allows designers to spec out interactions and motions.

BACK

In Summer 2017, I joined the Design Tools team at Facebook as a Product Design Intern. I was responsible for designing a feature for Origami Studio that would improve the handoff of prototypes between designers and engineers. I took the problem from a broad idea to high fidelity versions and prototyped the interactions to improve usability.
I worked closely with many designers and engineers but mainly my amazing mentor, Julius Tarng, who pushed me to grow as a designer and learn more about how to tackle a big problem and design a complete end-to-end feature.
