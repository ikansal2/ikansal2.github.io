HOMEFINDR.

Homefindr is a web application that allows the user to evaluate different neighborhoods in different cities based on factors like safety, affordability, nightlife and schooling. The application analysed zip codes from 4 major cities across the United States namely San Francisco, New York City, Chicago and Boston. The aim was to make it easier for people moving to a new city to find a good place to live that fits their needs.

AUDIORING

Audioring was a project that aimed to develop a visual fingerprint of a song based it's unique characteristics like notes, pitches, and amplitudes. The idea was to discover patterns between songs and see if anything can be said about the genre the song belonged to or the artist who produced it.

IMMIGRANT HUBS

Immigrant Hubs analysed census data to observe interesting patterns in where people from certain countries immigrate to in the United States and vice versa.
